About Rwanda

A nation of resilience, natural beauty, and extraordinary biodiversity in the heart of East Africa

THE LAND

Land of a Thousand Hills

Rwanda, a small landlocked nation in East Africa, captivates visitors with its stunning topography of rolling hills, volcanic mountains, and pristine lakes. Despite its compact size‚Äîroughly the size of Maryland‚ÄîRwanda packs extraordinary diversity into every square kilometer.

From the misty peaks of the Virunga Volcanoes in the north to the vast wetlands of Akagera in the east, from the ancient montane rainforests of Nyungwe to the serene shores of Lake Kivu in the west, Rwanda's landscapes are as varied as they are beautiful.

This geographic diversity creates habitats for an incredible array of wildlife, making Rwanda one of the world's premier destinations for eco-tourism and wildlife conservation.

URBAN RWANDA

Cities & Communities

Experience modern African cities that blend tradition with innovation

Kigali - The Capital

Clean, safe, and vibrant. Home to museums, markets, restaurants, and thriving arts scene. The organizational heart of Rwanda's economic growth.

Butare - Cultural Hub

Rwanda's intellectual center with universities, museums, and cultural institutions preserving and celebrating the nation's heritage.

Musanze - Adventure Gateway

Base for gorilla trekking and volcano hiking. A charming mountain town surrounded by dramatic volcanic peaks.
