Traditional Rwandan Cuisine

Discover the flavors that define Rwandan food culture

Ugali

A staple made from maize or cassava flour, served with vegetables, beans, or meat stews.

Brochettes

Grilled meat skewers, often goat or beef, marinated in local spices and served with plantains.

Isombe

Cassava leaves cooked with groundnuts, a traditional vegetable dish rich in flavor.

Fresh Fruits

Rwanda's tropical climate produces delicious pineapples, mangoes, passion fruits, and avocados.
